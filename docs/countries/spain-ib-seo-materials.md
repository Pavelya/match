# Study in Spain with the IB Diploma

### Official University Admission Guide for IB Students (2026)

**Last updated for the 2026 intake**

This guide explains **how the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma is used for university admission in Spain**, using only official public sources. It is written exclusively for **IB Diploma students and IB Coordinators**.

---

## How Spain Recognizes the IB Diploma

Spain officially recognizes the **International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma** as equivalent to the Spanish *T√≠tulo de Bachiller*. This equivalence allows IB students to access Spanish higher education without completing the national Spanish Bachillerato.

This recognition is established in Spanish education regulations published in the **Spanish Official State Gazette**, Spain‚Äôs official legal bulletin.

The accreditation process is administered by **UNEDassis**, the only public body authorized to validate international secondary school diplomas for university access in Spain.

**Official sources:**

* [https://www.boe.es](https://www.boe.es)
* [https://unedasiss.uned.es/home/acreditacion](https://unedasiss.uned.es/home/acreditacion)

---

## Do IB Students Need to Take Selectividad?

**No.**

IB students are **exempt from the general phase** of the Spanish university entrance examination (EvAU / Selectividad) due to the official equivalence of the IB Diploma.

This exemption is confirmed through UNEDassis accreditation and applies nationwide.

**Official sources:**

* [https://unedasiss.uned.es](https://unedasiss.uned.es)
* [https://www.universidades.gob.es/acceso-y-admision-a-la-universidad/](https://www.universidades.gob.es/acceso-y-admision-a-la-universidad/)

---

## How IB Scores Are Converted in Spain (0‚Äì14 System)

Spain uses a **two-step scoring system** regulated by the **Ministry of Universities**, with score conversion performed by UNEDassis.

---

### Base University Access Score (0‚Äì10)

Your total IB Diploma score (24‚Äì45 points) is converted into a **Base University Access Score** on a 0‚Äì10 scale.

| IB Points | Base Score |
| --------- | ---------- |
| 42‚Äì45     | 10.0       |
| 40        | ~9.5       |
| 36        | ~8.4       |
| 30        | ~6.8       |
| 24        | 5.0        |

**Official sources:**

* [https://unedasiss.uned.es](https://unedasiss.uned.es)
* [https://www.universidades.gob.es](https://www.universidades.gob.es)

---

### Subject Weighting Bonus (+0 to +4)

Universities may award up to **4 additional points** based on subject relevance (*ponderaciones*).

```
Base Score (0‚Äì10) + Weighted Subjects (up to +4) = Maximum 14
```

**Official sources:**

* [https://www.universidades.gob.es](https://www.universidades.gob.es)
* [https://www.comunidad.madrid/servicios/educacion/distrito-unico](https://www.comunidad.madrid/servicios/educacion/distrito-unico)

---

## How IB Subjects Increase Your Admission Score

Universities assign subject weightings of **0.1 or 0.2** depending on degree relevance.

Typical high-weight IB subjects:

* Engineering: Mathematics AA, Physics
* Health Sciences: Biology, Chemistry
* Business & Economics: Mathematics, Economics
* Humanities: History, Geography, Art

**Official sources:**

* [https://universitats.gencat.cat](https://universitats.gencat.cat)
* [https://www.comunidad.madrid/servicios/educacion](https://www.comunidad.madrid/servicios/educacion)

### Option 1: IB Higher Level Recognition

**Source:** [https://unedasiss.uned.es](https://unedasiss.uned.es)

## Public vs Private Universities for IB Students

### Public Universities

* Score-based admission
* Cut-off grades (*Nota de Corte*)
* UNEDassis required

**Source:** [https://www.universidades.gob.es](https://www.universidades.gob.es)

### Private Universities

* Holistic admissions
* UNEDassis often required for enrollment

---

## UNEDassis Documents Required for IB Students

* IB Diploma or predicted grades
* Academic transcripts
* Passport or ID
* Proof of payment
* Official IB results sent by the IBO

**Source:** [https://unedasiss.uned.es](https://unedasiss.uned.es)
