## Program 26: Computer Science BSc (Hons)

### Basic Info
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **URL** | https://study.ed.ac.uk/programmes/undergraduate/57-computer-science |
| **UCAS Code** | G400 |
| **Name** | Computer Science BSc (Hons) |
| **Degree Type** | BSc (Hons) |
| **Duration** | 4 years |
| **Field of Study** | Computer Science & IT |
| **School** | School of Informatics |

### Description (Exact from site)
> Computer science is about understanding, designing, implementing and using computing systems. These systems can range in scale, and complexity, from the tiny components of a single processor to the globe-spanning internet.

### IB Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum IB Points** | 34 (range: 34-43) |
| **HL Grades Required** | 6, 6, 5 (range: 665 to 777) |

### Subject Requirements

#### Higher Level (HL) - Required Subjects

| Subject | Level | Min Grade | Critical | Logic |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Mathematics AA ONLY** | HL | **6** | Yes | AND (required) |
| Any subject | HL | 6 | No | Any other subject |
| Any subject | HL | 5 | No | Any other subject |

⚠️ **IMPORTANT RESTRICTIONS:**
- **ONLY Math Analysis and Approaches (AA) accepted** - Math AI NOT accepted!
- Math HL at grade **6** required
- Math qualifications must be no more than **2 years old** at entry
- No prior programming experience needed

#### Standard Level (SL) - Required Subjects

| Subject | Level | Min Grade | Critical | Logic | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|-------|
| English | SL | 5 | No | AND | English ab initio not accepted |

### Additional Notes
- Ranked **23rd in the world** and **4th in the UK** for Computer Science (QS 2025)
- **Largest Informatics department in Europe**
- Study abroad available in Year 3 (Australia, USA, Canada, Europe, Singapore)
- No placement
- Can switch between Informatics programs after Year 1
- BCS (British Computer Society) does NOT accredit BSc - only BEng
- Difference from BEng: slightly less strict course selection, can still graduate if one honours course is failed with 40% average

## Program 29: Engineering BEng/MEng (Hons)

### Basic Info
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **URL** | https://study.ed.ac.uk/programmes/undergraduate/75-engineering |
| **UCAS Code** | H100 |
| **Name** | Engineering BEng/MEng (Hons) |
| **Degree Type** | BEng (Hons) / MEng (Hons) |
| **Duration** | 4 years (BEng) / 5 years (MEng) |
| **Field of Study** | Engineering |
| **School** | School of Engineering |

### Description (Exact from site)
> If you are unsure about which engineering discipline you are most interested in, this option allows you to explore which engineering discipline interests you the most and defer your choice of degree programme to before the start of Year 2. This programme does not lead to a general engineering degree. Instead, at the start of Year 2, you will join one of the following degree programmes: Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics and Electrical Engineering, Electrical and Mechanical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Structural and Fire Safety Engineering.

### IB Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum IB Points** | 34 (range: 34-37) |
| **HL Grades Required** | 6, 5, 5 (range: 655 to 666) |

### Subject Requirements

#### Higher Level (HL) - Required Subjects

| Subject | Level | Min Grade | Critical | Logic |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Mathematics AA ONLY** | HL | **5** | Yes | AND (required) |
| Physics OR Biology OR Chemistry OR CS OR Design Tech | HL | 5 | No | OR (one required) |
| Any subject | HL | 5 | No | Third HL |

⚠️ **IMPORTANT RESTRICTIONS:**
- **ONLY Math Analysis and Approaches (AA) accepted** - Math AI NOT accepted!
- Math HL at grade **5** required (lower than CS programs)
- **Applicants with Physics preferred**
- Science OR group: Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science, or Design Technology

#### Standard Level (SL) - Required Subjects

| Subject | Level | Min Grade | Critical | Logic | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|-------|
| Physics | SL | 5 | No | AND | **Only if not taking Physics at HL** |
| English | SL | 5 | No | AND | English ab initio not accepted |

### Additional Notes
- **Entry route program** - students choose specialization at start of Year 2
- Ranked **top 10 UK** for Engineering (THE & QS 2025)
- Ranked **1st in Scotland** for Engineering research (REF 2021)
- Study abroad available in Year 3
- Placements available (depends on chosen specialization)
- **Cannot join Structural Engineering with Architecture** - must apply directly in Year 1
- Transfer to Electronics and Computer Science possible on competitive basis
- Chemical Engineering only if prerequisite courses taken

### Year 2 Specializations Available
- Chemical Engineering BEng/MEng
- Civil Engineering BEng/MEng
- Electronics and Electrical Engineering BEng/MEng
- Electrical and Mechanical Engineering BEng/MEng
- Mechanical Engineering BEng/MEng
- Structural and Fire Safety Engineering BEng/MEng

  field: 'Engineering',
  degree: 'BEng (Hons)',
  duration: '4 years',
  minIBPoints: 34,
  programUrl: 'https://study.ed.ac.uk/programmes/undergraduate/75-engineering',
  requirements: [
    // HL Requirements - Math AA at grade 5

### Description (Exact from site)
> Our joint programmes allow you to study law alongside another academic discipline. Over four years of study, you will take a range of courses from both the Law School and the School of Social and Political Science. The joint LLB Law and Politics programme aims to promote advanced knowledge and understanding of the theory, concepts and rules of law. You will also gain an understanding of the choices that political systems must regularly make, the processes that maintain or change those systems, and the concepts and values used in political analysis.
